About The Budget Project

The Budget Project is an online platform dedicated to Romanian youth, focusing on financial education. The aim of the platform is to be a reliable source of personal finance management tips, with an emphasis on savings, investments and the creation of generational wealth in a way that is easily applicable for the target audience.

Laura Stanculescu, the creator of TBP is 21 years old and she studies Business Management at Queen Mary University of London, in Great Britain. Throughout the first semester of university, she has familiarized herself with finance concepts, thanks to the resources she had access to, both in and out of university. While looking for similar resources focused on Romania, Laura observed the little number of good quality resources and decided to start The Budget Project as a way to help courious young adults and teenagers with information relevant to the Romanian market. In the future, she hopes to start her own business with high ethical standards and social responsibility.
