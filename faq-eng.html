Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Budget Project?

The Budget Project is a platform of financial education dedicated to Romanian youth, that offers information that everyone should know about their personal finances.

How do I get in touch?

You can send us an email using the contact form available on the website or send us a message via Instagram Direct (@the.budgetproject).

What kind of information can I find on TBP?

On TBP we talk about various aspects of the financial world, from your personal budget, to entrepreneurial initiatives, investment methods, local businesses and investment portfolios of successful people.

What is the aim of TBP?

We aim to help you familiarize yourself with certain concepts and financial tools you can use to form healthy money habits.
